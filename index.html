What's killing your conversions

Three leaks I'll fix
in your store

Your current store probably has these. They're not obvious — but they compound.

📄

Product Pages That Confuse

Visitors don't understand what makes your product worth buying. No clear education hierarchy, buried benefits, and upsells that feel like traps. PDP clarity = checkout confidence.

🛒

A Cart Drawer That Breaks Flow

The moment someone adds to cart should accelerate the sale — not stall it. Most cart drawers miss upsell, skip AOV nudges, and give users zero reason to keep going.

📱

Mobile UX That Buries the Story

Over 70% of your traffic is mobile. If your product education, CTA hierarchy, and subscription/refill placement aren't designed mobile-first, you're leaking revenue daily.
